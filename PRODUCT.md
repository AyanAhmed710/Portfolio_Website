# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and AI/ML teams reviewing Ayan Ahmed's portfolio. Desktop-primary, scanning for technical depth and project credibility. Goal per visit: decide whether to reach out in under 3 minutes.

## Product Purpose

Personal portfolio for an AI/ML engineering student — showcasing project depth, leadership, and academic standing. Success = recruiter or hiring manager initiates contact within a day of visiting.

## Brand Personality

Sharp, technical, credible. Like a well-documented research repo with a great README — dense with signal, zero filler. The UI itself should demonstrate engineering competence.

## Anti-references

- Generic dark portfolios with gradient-text headings and identical icon-card grids
- Flat Notion-style white portfolios
- "Creative developer" particle-background sites
- Startup landing pages that happen to belong to a student

## Design Principles

1. Signal over noise — every element earns its space
2. The work is the hero — visuals illuminate projects, don't decorate them
3. Technical credibility — the interface style mirrors the domain (data viz, diagrams, precision)
4. Motion with intent — animations reveal or clarify, never distract
5. Consistency of voice over consistency of treatment — each section can have a different visual energy if the content demands it

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. Reduced motion respected. Optimized for 768px+ (desktop-primary audience).
